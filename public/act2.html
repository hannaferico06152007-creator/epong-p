Mae's Recipe for the Ppyeohaejangguk korean soup

Ingredients:

  • Pork Backbone-6 kls
  • Chinese petchay
  • Sibuyas
  • Denjang/Soybean-400 grams
  • Chili powder-400 grams
  • Mix Seasoning-300 grams
  • Ground Garlic-240 grams
  • Ground Ginger-72 grams

Procedures:

  1. Soak the meat for 4hours
  2. while soaking the meat,Boil the chinese petchay for 1hours
  3. After 4hours Wash the meat thoroughly
  4. Put the meat in the boiling water then let it boil for 10 minutes.then rinse it with water
  5. In a pot put the meat then add water and let it boil
  6. When it boils add denjang,garlic,and ginger and let it boil in medium heat
  7. Afer boiling add mix seasoning and chili powder in low heat
  8. Add chinese petchay then let it boil for 20 minutes in low heat
  9. Get the chinese petchay from the pot and let it strain
  10. Separate the meat and the soup(use strainer when you transfer the soup from the pot to the container etc.)

Setting:

  • 2 Slices of meat in 1 tokpeki
  • Add sliced chinese petchay
  • Add soup
  • Put to boil
  • Put sliced sibuyas
